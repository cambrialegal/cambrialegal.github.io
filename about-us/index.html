About Us

 

Cambria Legal is a boutique law firm servicing our locals in Western Sydney and surrounding regions.

At Cambria Legal we believe in providing practical and balanced advice that addresses your needs.

We explain in plain language every step of the legal process and the advantages and disadvantages of each of course of action.

We provide attentive and responsive client service. We listen to your needs and aim to provide sensible and lasting solutions.

Sometimes the best solution is a non-legal one that can save time and money. We will always tell you if this is the case. Our team is able to collectively draw upon a broad range of real world experience to help resolve your matter.

At Cambria Legal, we provide ourselves in ensuring all our clients, large or small, are serviced in a timely and efficient way that minimises costs and stress.

Our staff is friendly and approachable and has experience in dealing with a wide spectrum of clients, including those of culturally diverse backgrounds. Languages spoken in our firm include Greek, Thai, Vietnamese and Laos. We can also arrange interpreting services for other languages such as Mandarin, Khmer, Italian, Croatian and Arabic.

Cambria Legal also works closely with its business clients to understand their needs. We see ourselves as part of the team, protecting your business interests and ensuring your business is legally compliant in all areas.

When it can, Cambria Legal will also consider pro-bono (free-of-charge) services to eligible individuals and not-for-profit social enterprises in local communities. We believe in being an active member of our communities and giving back when possible.

Please contact us by phone or email, or just pop in and talk to us. We look forward to helping you.
